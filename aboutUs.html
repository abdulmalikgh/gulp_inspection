About Us

GULF INSPECTION SERVICES LTD is a Nigeria-owned professional independent inspection company with international technical partners.

Included in the Company’s scope of expertise is the verification and physical inspection of bulk cargoes including crude oil and gas, inspection, testing and Certification of manufactured and semi-manufactured products, shipping logistics/protocols and custody transfer documentation.

This work has been undertaken on behalf of a variety of clients, including both Government and Commercial organizations.

The Company has been extensively involved in quality and quantity superintendence throughout the World, at the point of manufacture, storage, transit and shipment.

This work has been undertaken on behalf of variety of clients, including both Government and Commercial organizations.
