What We do

Pre-shipment Inspection Agents under the Federal Ministry of Finance Nigerian Export Supervision Scheme (NESS), ensuring that the quality and quantity of exports correlate in all respects with Exporters’ declarations to the Federal Ministry of Finance and Central Bank.

Product Audit, Inspection, testing and Certification in regard to the following goods;

  • General Products (raw, semi-processed and processed)
  • Containerized Cargoes
  • Crude Oil (Inspection, Sampling & Verification)
  • Mechanical Equipment (components and finished products)
  • Cargo Damage Surveys
  • Vessel Draft, on-hire and off-hire surveys
  • Bulk Minerals & Ores
